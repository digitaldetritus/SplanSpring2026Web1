About Me: Biography

Laura Splan is a New York City based artist working at the intersections of Science, Technology, and Culture. Her research-based studio practice and interdisciplinary collaborations culminate in multimedia exhibitions and events. Through a process of co-creation and ideation, she has worked with physicists, biologists, virologists, epigeneticists, mycologists, and engineers. Recent projects have included immersive multisensory experiences, interactive installations, networked devices, participatory sculptures, and intimately scaled objects. With an approach grounded in what she calls the “tactical tactile”, her work cultivates intuitive comprehension of the interconnectedness of scientific phenomena and the everyday.
